## 🐕 Escolhendo Seu Pokémon

Choosing the right Pokémon is crucial for success in Hoenn. Here are some tips to help you pick the perfect companion:

- **Tipos Duplos**: Prioritize Pokémon with dual types for versatility in battle. For example, a Pokémon with both Fire and Water types can handle a wide range of opponents.
- **CP Alto**: Choose a Pokémon with a high CP to ensure you have a strong battle advantage.
- **Conjunto de Movimentos Forte**: Look for Pokémon with powerful moves that complement each other. For example, a Pokémon with a strong Fire move and a strong Water move can deal significant damage to a wide range of opponents.

## 🛡️ Estratégias de Batalha

Battles in Hoenn can be challenging, so it's important to have a solid strategy. Here are some tips to help you win more battles:

- **Use a Variety of Moves**: Don't rely on just one move. Use a mix of physical and special moves to keep your opponent guessing.
- **Know Your Pokémon's Weaknesses**: Avoid using moves that are ineffective against your Pokémon's weaknesses.
- **Use Items Wisely**: Save items like Potions and Super Potions for when you need them most.
