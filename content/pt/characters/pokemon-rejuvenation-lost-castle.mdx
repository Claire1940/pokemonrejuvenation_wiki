## 🏰 Descrição da Rota

Embark on an adventure to the mysterious Lost Castle in Pokémon Rejuvenation. This ancient structure is hidden from the main path and requires the Odd Key to access. The Lost Castle is filled with secrets, puzzles, and hidden items that await the brave explorers.

### Locais de Interesse

One of the key locations in the Lost Castle is the upper floors, which can be accessed using specific passwords: 0044, 6677, 3645, 9043, and 7171. These passwords unlock doors leading to valuable items and Pokémon encounters.

## 🏛️ Treinadores & Missões Paralelas

The Lost Castle is not just a place for treasure hunting; it also offers various trainers and sidequests. These encounters can provide additional challenges and rewards, enhancing your overall experience in Pokémon Rejuvenation.

### Missões Paralelas

- **Caça ao Tesouro**: Procure tesouros escondidos no castelo e ganhe itens valiosos.
- **Desafios de Puzzles**: Resolva quebra-cabeças para abrir áreas e itens secretos.
- **Criação de Pokémon**: Encontre Pokémon raros para criar e adicionar à sua coleção.
