<Section icon={Swords} title="Late-Game Powerhouses & Crest Synergies">

In the late-game chapters leading up to the Grand Dream City crisis and Chapter 16 routes, standard Pokémon are often outclassed by enemy teams with optimized IVs, EVs, and held items. To level the playing field, you must utilize two of Rejuvenation's unique mechanics: Crests and Aevian Forms.

Crests are unique held items scattered across Aevium that provide massive, tailor-made stat buffs or mechanical changes to specific underused Pokémon. For example, the Meganium Crest grants Meganium an additional Dragon typing and boosts its defensive capabilities, transforming a mediocre Grass-type starter into an elite late-game pivot. Similarly, Aevian Forms offer entirely new typing combinations and movepools that can catch late-game bosses off guard.
