<Section icon={Shield} title="Golett/Golurk Crest and Field Effect Synergies">

In Pokémon Rejuvenation, certain fully evolved Pokémon gain access to custom items called **Crests**. The **Golurk Crest** is a legendary held item that completely redefines how you use this Pokémon in late-game battles.

The Golurk Crest grants the following benefits:
- Increases the damage of all punching moves by 1.5x.
- Boosts physical bulk based on the active Field Effect.
- Restores a portion of HP when landing a successful contact move.

Because Golurk is slow, it relies heavily on Field Effects to survive and deal maximum damage. Many fields throughout Aevium naturally favor Ground and Ghost types.

### Field Effect Interactions

- **Haunted Field**: Boosts Ghost-type moves by 1.5x. Under this field, Golurk's Shadow Punch becomes an unstoppable force, especially when combined with the Iron Fist ability and the Golurk Crest.
- **Desert Field**: Increases the power of Ground-type moves like Earthquake and High Horsepower. Golurk becomes an excellent offensive pivot in this environment.
- **Wasteland Field**: Boosts Ground and Poison attacks while granting unique defensive interactions for heavy Ground-type physical attackers.
