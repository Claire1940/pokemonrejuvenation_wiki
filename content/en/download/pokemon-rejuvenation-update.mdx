<Section icon={Target} title="Help Center Quest Walkthroughs">

The Help Center remains the heart of progression in the **Pokemon Rejuvenation update**. Completing these requests is mandatory for players who wish to acquire rare Pokemon and essential utility items. The V14 release features several multi-part quests that require traveling between different apartment complexes and the Oceana Pier.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accepting the Missing Mother Request">
    Visit the Help Plaza board and accept the request from Sasha. She is located at the Petal Salon and is worried about her mother, Rosa, who hasn't returned from her hair appointments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Investigating the Apartments">
    Search for Rosa at the following locations: Aqua Apartments (Floor 2), Velvet Apartments, and Emerald Apartments. Speak to the clients in each building to track Rosa's movements toward Oceana Pier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Oceana Pier Confrontation">
    Travel to the storage facility at Oceana Pier. You will discover Rosa's secret salon for the less fortunate. Defeat the antagonist, Stella, in a battle to protect the operation and complete the quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claiming Rewards">
    Return to the Help Plaza to receive your reward. This quest grants a significant amount of money and unlocks the "Free Haircut" service at the salon.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Time-Sensitive Quests">
Some quests, such as "Wretched Music," are only available during the night (after 8:00 PM in-game time). Check your Pokegear clock frequently to ensure you don't miss these specific windows.
</Callout>
