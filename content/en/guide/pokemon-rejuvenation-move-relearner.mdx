<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating the Mirage Woods Puzzle">

Mirage Woods is notorious for its illusory paths and confusing layout. The **pokemon rejuvenation move relearner** is hidden deep within a cave that is only accessible by following a specific route through these woods.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Route 3">
    From Sheridan Village, head left toward Route 3. Enter the Mirage Woods entrance. Note that the layout may seem to loop if you take the wrong turn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Illusion">
    Go through the initial tree line and head left. You will pass a tent and a trainer. Look for a cuttable tree on a rock ledge; use Cut to clear the path and cross over.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Disappearing Bridge">
    Head down and right. You will encounter a bridge that disappears as you approach. Instead of crossing where the bridge was, cut the nearby tree and head down to find the alternate path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Surf Segment">
    Once you reach the area with the waterfall, head to the far left. You will see a series of "fake" trees that you can walk through. Go behind the waterfall area and use **Surf** to cross the small pond.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Hidden Cave">
    After surfing across, you will find a small cave entrance. Inside is Samantha's sister, the Move Relearner. Speak to her to fix the situation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Navigation Point | Action Required | Reward/Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Route 3 Entrance** | Head West from Sheridan | Entry point |
| **Rock Ledge** | Use HM Cut | Path progression |
| **Left Path (Secret)** | Search behind trees | **Zora Quest** start |
| **Waterfall Pond** | Use HM Surf | Access to Relearner Cave |

<Callout type="tip" title="Secret Encounter">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Heart Scale Farming Strategies">

Accessing the **pokemon rejuvenation move relearner** is only half the battle. To actually use her services, you need Heart Scales. In the Aevium region, these are not as common as in official titles, so you must be strategic in how you acquire them.

  <Card title="Mining Rocks">

    - Use the Mining Kit on glowing rocks
    - High RNG dependence
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pickup Ability">
    - **Passive farming**
    - Use Meowth or Teddiursa
    - Scales with level (1% - 10% chance)

  <Card title="Wild Pokemon">
    - **Consistent farming**

  </Card>

The most reliable way to get Heart Scales in 2026 is through the **Mining Kit**. As you explore caves like Amethyst Grotto or Caratorn Forest, always check the walls. If you are desperate, fishing for Luvdisc with a Pokemon that knows "Thief" (such as Mightyena or Gothitelle) is the fastest active farming method.

| Method | Success Rate | Effort Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mining Kit** | Moderate (15% per node) | Low |
| **Pickup (Level 71-100)** | Low (10% per battle) | Passive |
| **Wild Luvdisc (Thief)** | **High (50% with Compound Eyes)** | High |
| **Samantha's Reward** | Guaranteed (1 Scale) | One-time |
