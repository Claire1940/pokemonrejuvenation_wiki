<Section icon={Target} title="The Missing Mother Walkthrough">

The Missing Mother quest is one of the most narrative-heavy early side quests in East Gearen City. It introduces you to Sasha, a local salon worker whose mother, Rosa, has gone missing after a series of private appointments. To complete this quest successfully, you must investigate several apartment complexes across the city before tracking Rosa down to a remote location.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Request">
    Visit the Help Plaza board in East Gearen City and accept the **Missing Mother** request posted by Sasha. Head to the local Hair Salon to speak with her and initiate the search.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Investigate Aqua Apartments">
    Go to the Aqua Apartments and head up to the second floor. Speak to the female NPC wearing a pink dress. She reveals that Rosa completed her hair appointment but left quickly to visit the Velvet Apartments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Search Velvet Apartments">
    Navigate to the Velvet Apartments and make your way to the third floor. Speak to the resident with long brown hair. She confirms Rosa was there but rushed out to her final appointment at the Emerald Apartments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visit Emerald Apartments & Oceana Pier">
    Enter the lobby of the Emerald Apartments. The receptionist informs you that Rosa accidentally left her hairbrush behind and mentioned heading down to **Oceana Pier** to take care of some urgent business.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Defeat Blackmailer Stella">
    Head to Oceana Pier and enter the storage facility. You will discover that Rosa runs a secret, free salon for the homeless population of Grand Dream City. Defeat Stella in a battle to stop her from blackmailing Rosa's operation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Rorrim B & The Wretched Music Battle Strategy">

Once night falls in East Gearen City (8:00 PM in-game time), a new request titled **Wretched Music** becomes available at the Help Plaza. The receptionist at the Chrysola Hotel complains of loud, bizarre music shaking the hotel's foundation from the basement. Investigating this disturbance leads to an encounter with the eccentric, music-loving trainer Rorrim B.

To resolve the situation peacefully, you must first battle Rorrim B and then assist him in finding two new members to join his dance troop.

| Rorrim B's Pokemon | Level | Primary Type | Strategy & Counters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Litleo** | 8 | Fire / Normal | Use Water or Fighting-type moves like Double Kick. |
| **Rockruff** | 8 | Rock | Vulnerable to Grass, Water, Fighting, and Steel. |
| **Passimian** | 8 | Fighting | High physical attack. Use Flying or Psychic moves. |

To satisfy Rorrim B's requirements and get him to leave the hotel basement, locate these two aspiring dancers hidden within the Chrysola Hotel:

1. **The Aspiring Dancer**: Found in one of the standard hotel rooms. She immediately expresses her desire to escape her boring routine and join the troop.
2. **The Bellhop**: Located near the main lobby. He admits to feeling a deep, spiritual connection to Rorrim B's rhythmic beats and leaves his post to dance.

<Callout type="success" title="Quest Turn-In Reward">
After recruiting both members, return to the hotel basement to watch their transformation. Rorrim B will happily depart, and the hotel receptionist will reward you. Claim your final payout at the Help Plaza counter.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Rooftop Showdown: Battling Trainer Nove">

For players looking to test their tactical limits, a wandering trainer named **Nove** awaits on the rooftop of the Chrysola Hotel. She challenges you to a high-stakes battle with a recommended level of 20. Because this fight sits slightly above the early-game level cap of 18, utilizing status conditions and clever typing is essential to secure victory.

| Nove's Pokemon | Level | Key Behaviors | Recommended Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rockruff** | 20 | Fast physical attacker | Combusken with Double Kick |
| **Passimian** | 20 | Heavy physical damage | Shuppet with Will-O-Wisp |

To defeat Nove, lead with a Pokemon capable of inflicting status conditions. A wild-caught Shuppet utilizing **Will-O-Wisp** is highly effective here, as burning Passimian cuts its massive physical attack power in half. Alternatively, utilizing Voltorb's **Spark** to land a paralysis can swing the speed advantage back in your favor, allowing your starter Pokemon to clean up the remaining threats.

<Callout type="warning" title="Level Cap Restriction">
Remember that Pokemon Rejuvenation enforces a strict level cap system. If your Pokemon exceed the current cap of 18 before defeating the first Gym Leader, they will disobey your commands in battle.
</Callout>
