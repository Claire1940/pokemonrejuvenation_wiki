<Section icon={Terminal} title="Step-by-Step: Enabling the Debug Menu">

For those who need more than just passwords, enabling the full **pokemon rejuvenation debug mode** menu requires a small modification to the game's internal files. This menu grants you the "God Mode" of Aevium, allowing you to walk through walls, set your own weather, and manipulate relationship values with NPCs like Melia or Ren.

Follow these steps carefully to ensure you don't break your V14 installation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Backup Your Save Files">
    Before touching any game data, locate your save folder (usually in `C:\Users\[User]\Saved Games\Pokemon Rejuvenation`). Copy the entire folder to a separate drive. V14 saves are not compatible with older V13.5 scripts, so ensure your backup is current.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Scripts File">
    Navigate to your main Pokemon Rejuvenation V14 directory. Open the `Data` folder. You are looking for a file named `Scripts.rxdata`. Note that in some versions, this might be bundled or hidden; if so, you may need a dedicated "Debug Enabler" script provided by the community on the Reborn Evolved forums.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply the Debug Edit">
    Using a script editor or a pre-modified debug script file, you must set the `$DEBUG` variable to `true`. Alternatively, many players use the "Modular Workshop" mod which adds a toggleable Debug option directly into the game's Options menu without manual file editing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify in Game">
    Launch the game. Open your main menu (where you see Pokémon, Bag, Save). If successful, a new "Debug" option will appear at the bottom of the list. You can also press F9 while in the overworld to open the Quick Debug menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Wand2} title="Key Debug Menu Functions">

Once you have enabled the full **pokemon rejuvenation debug mode**, you will have access to hundreds of commands. For most players, only a few are actually necessary for day-to-day gameplay.

### Overworld Commands
- **Warp**: Teleport to any map ID in the game. Note that Aevium is divided into many sub-maps (Grand Dream City has dozens).
- **Set Metadata**: Change your player character's appearance or name on the fly.
- **Switches & Variables**: This is the most powerful tool. You can manually toggle "Switch 001" to tell the game you have already defeated a specific Gym Leader or met a specific NPC.

### Pokémon Management
- **Add Pokémon**: Generate any Pokémon from Gen 1 to Gen 9, including Aevian Forms.
- **Fill Pokédex**: Instantly complete your local and national dex entries.
- **Modify Party**: Change levels, abilities, movesets, and even "Shiny" status with a single click.
