<Section icon={Target} title="Understanding Ability Capsules in V14">

In the challenging world of Aevium, the **pokemon rejuvenation ability capsule** is more than just a convenience; it is a vital tool for survival. Unlike official titles where difficulty is often negligible, Pokémon Rejuvenation V14 demands that players optimize every aspect of their team to overcome Gym Leaders and Team Xen executives who utilize competitive-grade strategies.

The Ability Capsule functions by toggling a Pokémon between its two natural ability slots. If a species only has one natural ability, the capsule will have no effect. It is important to distinguish these from **Ability Patches**, which are rarer items used specifically to transition a Pokémon from a standard ability to its Hidden Ability (HA).

| Item Type | Primary Function | Rarity | Reversibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ability Capsule** | Swaps between natural Ability 1 and Ability 2 | Common (Late-game) | Fully Reversible |
| **Ability Patch** | Unlocks the Hidden Ability slot | Rare | Requires another Patch to revert |
| **Hidden Ability Capsule** | Swaps between multiple Hidden Abilities (if applicable) | Very Rare | Limited |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
Before using a capsule, check the **Field Effect** of the upcoming boss. An ability like **Dry Skin** might be a liability in a standard battle but becomes S-tier in a Rain-based or Water-surface field.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step: How to Use the Ability Capsule">

Using the **pokemon rejuvenation ability capsule** is straightforward, but there are a few prerequisites to ensure you don't waste the item on a Pokémon that cannot change its traits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Verify Ability Slots">
    Open your Pokémon's summary page. Check online or in the Pokédex to see if the species has a second natural ability. If it only has one (e.g., Flygon's Levitate), the capsule will not work.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Medicine Pocket">
    Open your Bag and navigate to the **Medicine** or **General** pocket (depending on your V14 UI settings). Locate the Ability Capsule.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply to Pokémon">
    Select 'Use' and choose the target Pokémon. A prompt will appear asking if you want to change the ability. Confirm the selection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm the Change">
    Re-examine the summary page. The new ability should be active immediately. This change is permanent until another capsule is used.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Hidden Ability Warning">
If you use a standard Ability Capsule on a Pokémon with a **Hidden Ability**, it will revert to one of the two **Standard Abilities**. You will need an **Ability Patch** to get the Hidden Ability back.
</Callout>
