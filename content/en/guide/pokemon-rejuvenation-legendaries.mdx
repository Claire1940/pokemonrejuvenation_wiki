<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Catching Strategy">

Capturing **pokemon rejuvenation legendaries** requires a disciplined approach. You cannot simply rely on False Swipe and a Sleep move from turn one. Follow this optimized 2026 procedure to ensure you don't waste your resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Shield Depletion">
    Focus on your highest damage output. Use super-effective moves to break through the 2-4 shield bars. During this phase, the legendary will often use "Unleashed Power," a massive AOE attack that triggers after a shield breaks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Terrain Neutralization">
    Once the shields are low, use moves like Cloud Nine, Ice Spinner, or your own Terrain/Weather moves. Removing the legendary's field advantage is crucial for surviving their final stand.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Status Application">
    After the final shield bar is gone, the legendary becomes vulnerable to status conditions. Sleep (Spore) or Paralysis (Thunder Wave) are mandatory. Note that some legendaries may have abilities like Inner Focus or Vital Spirit, so plan accordingly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Capture Phase">
    With the boss at 1 HP (via False Swipe) and asleep, start using Ultra Balls or specialized balls like Dusk Balls (if at night) or Timer Balls (if the fight has lasted 10+ turns).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Detailed Boss Breakdown: Raikou, Entei, and Suicune">

Each member of the legendary trio requires a different tactical mindset. In Pokemon Rejuvenation, these aren't just palette swaps; their AI and movepools are tailored to their specific environments.

### Raikou: The Lightning Strike
Raikou is the fastest of the three. In Alamissa, it utilizes the Electric Terrain to double its speed and power up its Electric-type moves.

| Move | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rising Voltage** | Electric | Double damage on Terrain |
| **Calm Mind** | Psychic | Boosts Sp. Atk and Sp. Def |
| **Extra Sensory** | Psychic | Coverage against Fighting/Poison |
| **Extreme Speed** | Normal | Priority finishing move |

### Entei: The Volcanic Guardian
Entei thrives in the heat. Its Sacred Fire has a high burn rate, which can quickly whittle down your physical attackers.

| Move | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sacred Fire** | Fire | 50% Burn chance |
| **Stone Edge** | Rock | High Crit coverage |
| **Extreme Speed** | Normal | Priority |
| **Flare Blitz** | Fire | Massive recoil damage |

### Suicune: The North Wind
Suicune is the most defensive. If allowed to set up multiple Calm Minds in the rain, it becomes nearly unkillable.

| Move | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Scald** | Water | High Burn chance |
| **Calm Mind** | Psychic | Defensive setup |
| **Ice Beam** | Ice | Coverage against Grass/Dragon |
| **Mirror Coat** | Psychic | Punishes special attackers |

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Strategy">
Use a Pokemon with the "Unaware" ability (like Quagsire or Clefable) to ignore Suicune's Calm Mind boosts. This allows you to chip away at its health without worrying about its rising special stats.
</Callout>

</Section>
