<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Pokedex Completion Strategy">

Completing the regional directory requires a methodical approach. Because the game features a soft level cap system tied to gym badges, you cannot simply over-level a single monster to steamroll encounters. Instead, you must build a versatile roster by catching utility monsters early.

Follow this optimized path to maximize your catch rate and secure rare encounters as early as possible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your Early Utility Team">
    Catch a **Caterpie** or **Weedle** in the Chrisola Hotel or Route 1. Fully evolving them into Butterfree or Beedrill by Level 10 provides early-game status moves like Sleep Powder, which dramatically increases your catch success rate on wild targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hunt for Shadow Pokemon">
    Participate in Dimensional Rift battles and clean up Shadow Pokemon throughout Gearen Alleyway and Goldenwood Forest. Purifying these monsters unlocks exclusive moves and adds highly sought-after entries to your save file.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect and Revive Fossils">
    Use the Mining Kit on glowing rock walls to extract rare fossils. Bring these fossils to the specialized laboratories in West Gearen City or Akuwa Town to resurrect ancient powerhouses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check Time and Weather Cycles">
    Certain species, like Gastly in the Wispy Chasm or Alolan Marowak, only spawn at night. Use the in-game PokeGear to monitor weather patterns, as rainy conditions unlock distinct water-type encounters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Irreversible Choices">
Some event Pokemon, such as the gift Pichu in East Gearen City or the trade Alolan Vulpix in Kristiline Town, are one-time opportunities. Accidental knockouts or refusing the trade will permanently lock them out for that playthrough.
</Callout>

</Section>
