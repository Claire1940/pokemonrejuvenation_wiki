<Section icon={Trophy} title="Steps to Acquire Rare Mystery Eggs">

Located at the Route 3 Traveling Fair, this event rewards players with a random egg containing a rare, high-potential Pokémon. The exact Pokémon you hatch is determined when you receive the egg, making it a highly anticipated milestone for team optimization.

To successfully claim and hatch your Mystery Egg, follow this structured progression route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Route 3">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Egg Vendor">
    Find the specialized vendor offering the Mystery Egg transaction in the center of the fairgrounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prepare the Exchange">
    Ensure you have the required currency or trade items requested by the vendor to secure the egg.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Hatch and Reveal">
