The secret room puzzle involves three music tiles located on the stage. These tiles correspond to numbers 4, 3, and 2 from left to right. To unlock the path, you must input two specific sets of combinations obtained from trainers within the gym.

| Tile Position | Number Value | Associated Trainer Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Left Tile** | 4 | Left door, end of the hallway |
| **Middle Tile** | 3 | Used in both combinations |
| **Right Tile** | 2 | Right side trainer |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Unlocking the Secret Stage Room">

Following the 13.5 update, many players find themselves stuck at Venam's gym trying to replicate old strategies. Follow these precise steps to unlock the secret stage area and reach the Gearen Alleyway.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Music Tiles">
    After clearing the initial gym trainers, head to the main stage. You will see three distinct tiles on the floor. Stepping on them triggers a number display.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Input the First Combination">
    Step on the tiles in the following order: **2, 3, 3, 2**. This sequence is obtained from the trainer on the right side of the gym. No visual cue will trigger yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Input the Second Combination">
    Immediately after the first set, input: **4, 4, 3, 3, 2**. This sequence is found via the trainer behind the left door. Once completed, the door behind the stage will open.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate to Gearen Alleyway">
    Enter the newly opened door and use the right teleporter. This leads you to the hidden Gearen Alleyway section where you can find unique encounters and the secret egg.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Boss | Primary Type | Key Field Effect | Best Counters |
