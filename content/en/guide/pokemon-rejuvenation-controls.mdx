<Section icon={Shield} title="Gamepad Setup and Controller Configuration">

Playing with a controller offers a more comfortable, console-like experience. While the game client has basic native controller detection, modern XInput controllers (like Xbox Series X or PlayStation 5 gamepads) often require a mapping utility to function correctly without input latency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Connect Your Controller">
    Ensure your controller is connected to your PC via USB or Bluetooth before launching the Pokemon Rejuvenation executable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Add to Steam Library">
    Open Steam, click **Add a Game** in the bottom-left corner, and select **Add a Non-Steam Game**. Browse and select the Pokemon Rejuvenation launcher.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enable Steam Input">
    Launch Steam in Big Picture Mode, navigate to the game, and open the Controller Layout settings. Enable Steam Input to map your gamepad buttons directly to the default keyboard keys.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Alternative Mapping Software">
    If you do not use Steam, download a third-party utility like **JoyToKey** or **Keysticks**. Map your controller's D-pad to the keyboard's Arrow Keys, the 'A' button to 'C', and the 'B' button to 'X'.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Input Overlap">
Avoid running multiple mapping utilities simultaneously. Running Steam Input alongside JoyToKey can cause double-input errors, making menu navigation difficult.
</Callout>

</Section>
