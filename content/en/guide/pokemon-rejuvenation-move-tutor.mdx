<Section icon={Swords} title="Navigating the Mirage Woods Puzzle">

To locate the **pokemon rejuvenation move tutor**, you must first enter the house on the far right of Sheridan Village. While this is her eventual home, she is missing at the start of V13.5. You must backtrack to Route 3 and enter the Mirage Woods. This area is notorious for its shifting paths and "disappearing" bridges that can confuse even veteran players.

The puzzle relies on interacting with specific trees and triggers that change the environment. If you find yourself looping back to the entrance, you likely missed a "Cut" tree or a hidden path behind a tent.

| Location | Action | Key Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Route 3** | Head west from Sheridan | Entrance to Mirage Woods |
| **Mirage Woods Tent** | Battle the nearby trainer | Clears path to the upper ledge |
| **The Rock Bridge** | Use Cut on the northern tree | Crosses the first major gap |
| **Zora Sidequest** | Head left after the bridge | Optional quest for Zora |
| **Mirage Town** | Cross the lower bridge | Final hub before the tutor |

<Callout type="warning" title="Disappearing Bridges">
In Mirage Woods, some bridges will disappear once you cross them or interact with specific trees. If you get stuck, try cutting any nearby small trees to reset the pathing or open a shortcut back to the main trail.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Move Relearner Quest">

Follow these precise steps to find the NPC and bring her back to Sheridan Village. This process is mandatory to enable the **pokemon rejuvenation move tutor** services in the village house.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Sheridan Village House">
    Go to the house on the right in Sheridan Village. An automatic cutscene may trigger if it is your first time. Note that the house is currently empty.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Mirage Woods">
    Exit Sheridan, head to Route 3, and enter Mirage Woods. Use Cut on the first tree you see and head left toward the trainer tent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Solve the Bridge Puzzle">
    After crossing the initial rock bridge, go down and right. A bridge will disappear, forcing you to use Cut on a tree to the south. Follow this path into the area known as Mirage Town.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Hidden Waterfall Path">
    In Mirage Town, walk behind the waterfall. Head to the left and look for a gap in the trees. You won't see a clear path, so keep walking right until you hit the water's edge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Surf to the NPC">
    Use Surf and travel through the hidden tree gap. Speak to the woman standing on the small island. Once the dialogue finishes, she will return to Sheridan Village.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Quest Completion">
Once the NPC says she is returning home, you can simply use an Escape Rope or Fly back to Sheridan Village. She will now be standing behind the counter in the Move Relearner's house.
</Callout>

</Section>
