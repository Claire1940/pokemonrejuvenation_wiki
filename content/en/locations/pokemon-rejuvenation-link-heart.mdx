<Section icon={Target} title="Earth's Heart Walkthrough: Garufian Ruins Puzzle">

The journey to the Earth's Heart is one of the most challenging segments in the mid-game. Located beneath Cotos Mountain, this area forces players to solve puzzles reminiscent of the Hoenn Regi chambers. You will need to coordinate with NPCs like Alita and the reformed Team Xen grunt, Tommen, to disable the ancient seals.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Navigate the Poisonous Dump">
    Descend into the lower levels of Cotos Mountain. You will encounter toxic hazards and lava flows. Ensure your team is healed after battling the Maids of Angie.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Decode the Braille Seals">
    Reach the Garufian ruins. The first seal requires you to read the Braille and perform a specific action—usually running around the perimeter of the room and using the move **Flash** or a Taser item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Statue Alignment Puzzle">
    In the secondary chamber, you must move statues of Latias, Sabaleye, Ninetales, and Lunatone into their respective corners. Follow the visual cues on the floor tiles to match the ancient symbols.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dual Platform Activation">
    Coordinate with Alita and Tommen. Two people must stand on the pressure plates in separate rooms simultaneously to unlock the final gate leading to the Earth's Heart.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Reward">
Solving the statue alignment puzzle correctly grants access to a hidden chest containing a **Link Heart** and a TM for Stomping Tantrum.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Defeating the Earth's Heart Guardian: Groudon">

The climax of the Earth's Heart quest is a brutal confrontation with a level-scaled Groudon. This battle is unique because the terrain heavily buffs Fire and Ground moves while penalizing Water-type attacks due to the permanent **Drought** ability.

  <Card title="Phase 1: Standard Groudon">

    - **Key Threats**: Heat Crash and Precipice Blades.
    - **Strategy**: Use **Intimidate** cycles to lower its Attack.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Phase 2: Primal Reversion">
    - **Ability**: Desolate Land (Water moves fail completely).

  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info" title="Battle Mechanic">
In the Garufian Ruins terrain, moves like **Body Slam** and **Storm Throw** receive a significant power boost. Use Pokemon like Mawile or Machamp to capitalize on these buffs.
</Callout>
