<Section icon={Swords} title="Amethyst Cave Trainer Battles & Gang Showdown">

Once inside the cave, you will notice members of the rival gang causing trouble. If you spoke to Karina beforehand, you can interfere in their operations. The cave consists of standard trainers and a final confrontation with the Gang Boss. Navigating these battles requires careful planning, as the opposing teams utilize solid coverage moves.

The first major obstacle is a gang member blocking the path. He utilizes a Steel-type team featuring Magnemite and Klink. Ground or Fire-type moves make quick work of his roster. After defeating him, you can advance deeper to find the Gang Boss. The boss uses a combination of Fighting and Water-type Pokémon, including Qwilfish and Mankey. Defeating him completes Karina's quest, prompting the gang to retreat. As a token of gratitude, Karina rewards you with a Houndour.

| Trainer | Pokémon Team | Key Counters & Types | Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gang Grunt** | Klink (Lv. 18) | Fire, Ground, Fighting | Green Shard |
| **Gang Boss** | Qwilfish (Lv. 18), Mankey (Lv. 19) | Psychic, Flying, Electric | Houndour |
| **Hiker** | Geodude (Lv. 17), Roggenrola (Lv. 17) | Water, Grass, Fighting | EXP & Pokédollars |

<Callout type="warning" title="Level Cap Warning">
Ensure your Pokémon do not exceed the level cap during these battles. Exceeding the cap causes your Pokémon to disobey commands in battle, which can easily turn a winning matchup into a defeat.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Amethyst Cave Hidden Items & Wild Encounters">

The Amethyst Cave is filled with valuable items, many of which are hidden in rock formations or require thorough exploration. While navigating the main pathways, keep an eye out for interactable rocks that contain rare items. 

Some items, such as the Zygarde Cell, are visible but locked behind environmental barriers. You must return to this cave later in the game once you obtain the Surf HM to collect them. For now, focus on gathering the immediate items scattered across the main floor.

| Item Name | Location Description | Accessibility | Purpose / Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mineral Ball** | Hidden in a rock on the upper floor | Immediate | Specialized Pokéball for cave-dwelling Pokémon |
| **Green Shard** | Near the first gang member battle | Immediate | Trade currency for rare items or tutors |
| **Electric Gem** | Located near the Gang Boss arena | Immediate | Boosts the power of a single Electric-type move |
| **Reverse Candy** | Found on the lower path near the exit | Immediate | Lowers a Pokémon's level by one |
| **Zygarde Cell** | Visible on the lower water edge | Requires Surf HM | Used to assemble Zygarde later in the game |
| **X Attack** | Hidden on a ledge near the exit | Immediate | Temporarily boosts Attack in battle |
| **Rock Gem** | Hidden in a rock near the exit | Immediate | Boosts the power of a single Rock-type move |

The wild encounters in the cave are excellent for expanding your Pokédex. You can encounter Carbink and Loudred in the cave's rustling spots and standard walking areas. Carbink is a solid defensive option if your team lacks a sturdy Rock/Fairy-type wall.

| Pokémon | Level Range | Encounter Type | Recommended Catch Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Carbink** | Lv. 15 - 18 | Cave Ground | Use status conditions like Sleep or Paralysis to secure |
| **Loudred** | Lv. 16 - 19 | Cave Ground | Reduce HP to red; watch out for its high-damage Normal moves |

<Callout type="tip" title="Item Hunting Tip">

<Section icon={Target} title="Nim's Challenge: Three Games & Apprentice Battle">

Near the exit of the cave, you will encounter Nim, a mysterious and energetic traveler. Before she allows you to pass through to Sheridan Village, she forces you to participate in three distinct games. These games serve as both a puzzle and a strength test.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Purple Pebble Game">
    Nim asks you to locate a special purple pebble hidden inside a rock. Interact with the nearby Purple Rock to break it down. There is no trick here; breaking the designated rock always yields the pebble.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Fake Tree Game">
    Nim challenges you to identify a fake tree among a group of real ones. Inspect the trees carefully. The fake tree stands out due to its slight structural differences. Interact with it to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Apprentice Nim Battle">
    The final game is a direct battle. Nim challenges you with a well-balanced team. Defeat her to prove your strength and complete her trial.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Nim's battle team consists of Darumaka and Solrock. Darumaka is a fast, hard-hitting physical attacker, while Solrock acts as a bulky psychic and rock threat. Setting up Light Screen or using defensive switches is highly recommended to mitigate their damage.

| Opponent | Pokémon | Level | Type | Key Counters & Tactics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Apprentice Nim** | Darumaka | Lv. 18 | Fire | Use Water or Rock-type moves. Beware of its high physical Attack. |
| **Apprentice Nim** | Solrock | Lv. 19 | Rock/Psychic | Counter with Grass, Water, or Ghost-type attacks. |

After defeating Nim, she will ask you a final question: "Did I cheer you up?" Answering honestly completes the encounter. Nim then reveals that she retrieved Melia's Room ID Card from the depths of the rift where Melia's bag fell. She hands the card over to you before sending you on your way to Sheridan Village.

<Callout type="info" title="Story Context">
Nim's dialog reveals important lore details regarding the aftermath of the battle with Zeta. The Room ID Card she gives you is a vital key item that unlocks Melia's personal quarters back in East Gearen.
</Callout>

</Section>

Upon exiting the Amethyst Cave, you will arrive at Sheridan Village. The village is currently experiencing tension, as the local Eldest explains that the Sensei has locked himself in his house due to depression, neglecting his duties. 

While you must progress the main story to resolve the village's issues, there are several immediate activities and side quests you can partake in.

| Activity / Quest | NPC / Location | Requirements | Reward / Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Audino Training** | Training Trainer | None | Repeatable battle for fast EXP farming |

After exploring the village, it is highly recommended to use the truck service to return to East Gearen. Now that you possess Melia's Room ID Card, you can access her locked room. Inside, you will find incredibly valuable progression items, including the highly sought-after XP Share.
