Navigating the **pokemon rejuvenation aquamarine cave** requires a systematic approach. The cave is divided into three distinct zones: the Entryway, the Boulder Chambers, and the Flooded Sanctuary. Follow these steps to clear the cave and solve its environmental puzzles efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Entryway and Navigate Route 3">
    Enter the cave from the eastern path of Route 3. The upper ledge contains basic wild encounters and a single visible item ball. Walk along the natural stone ramp, descending toward the lower level where the sound of rushing water becomes louder.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Solve the Strength Boulder Puzzle">
    Approach the northern wall where three large grey boulders block the path to the lower chambers. Use a Pokémon with **HM Strength** to push the central boulder forward two spaces, then push the left boulder into the adjacent water gap to create a solid stone bridge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cross the Flooded Subterranean Lake">
    Once the boulder bridge is secure, walk to the water's edge. Interact with the dark blue water and use **HM Surf**. Swim directly north to navigate around the rocky pillars, keeping an eye out for floating item logs containing rare evolution stones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Hidden Deep Chamber">
    In the northeastern corner of the flooded lake, locate a narrow dry ledge. Press the hidden stone switch on the back wall to drain a small portion of the pool, revealing a lower staircase that leads directly into the Deep Sanctuary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The entire lower floor of the cavern is governed by the **Water Surface Field Effect**. This field alters standard type matchups and move powers, making standard ground-based strategies highly risky.

Electric-type moves are incredibly potent here, as they electrify the water surface, dealing continuous damage to non-insulated combatants. Conversely, Fire-type moves suffer a severe damage penalty, making your Fire-type starter choice highly vulnerable during cave battles.
