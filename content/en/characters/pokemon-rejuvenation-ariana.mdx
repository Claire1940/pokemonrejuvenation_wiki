<Section icon={Swords} title="Battle Strategies and Team Composition">

Defeating Ariana requires a balanced team that can handle her high-speed offensive threats. Because she favors Dark and Poison types, Fairy and Ground-type Pokemon are your best friends. However, in V14, many of her Pokemon carry "coverage moves" specifically designed to counter these weaknesses (such as Iron Head on her Dark-types to hit Fairy-types).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Field">
    Before attacking, check the Field Effect tooltip. If the field boosts Dark-type moves by 1.5x, your first priority should be using a move like **Nature Power** or **Mist** to alter or neutralize the terrain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Lead">
    Ariana often leads with a fast "hazard setter" or a Pokemon with U-turn/Volt Switch. Use a lead Pokemon with the **Inner Focus** ability or a Priority move to disrupt her momentum.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage the Level Cap">
    Ensure your team is at the maximum allowed level for your current badge count. Ariana’s AI is optimized to exploit even a 2-level disadvantage, especially on Intense difficulty.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Counter the Ace">
    Her "Ace" Pokemon usually holds a Mega Stone or a Z-Crystal in later chapters. Save your own Mega Evolution or Z-Move specifically to trade hits with her strongest team member.
  </Step>
</Steps>
