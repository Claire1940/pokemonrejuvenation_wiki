<Section icon={Target} title="Darchlight Caves Walkthrough & Mirror Puzzle">

The proper introduction to Erin's gameplay arc begins when the player, along with the Grass-type Gym Leader Flora and her brother Florin, falls into the subterranean depths of the Darchlight Caves. Upon waking up, the group meets Erin, who has been searching for her missing friend, Aelita. 

To escape the caves, the group decides to split up. The player teams up with Erin to explore the right path, while Florin takes the middle path and Flora takes the left path. Along the right path, you will discover an abandoned Blade Star facility. After interacting with a computer terminal and defeating a Blade Star grunt, Erin deduces that Flora is secretly affiliated with Blade Star, as she deliberately chose the left path to cover her tracks.

To proceed further, you must assist Erin in solving the Darchlight Mirror puzzle to clear the path.

| Step | Action Item | Input Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Mine Black Ore | Locate the ore deposit in the cave and smelt it in the left furnace |
| **2** | Second Button | Press the Blue button |
| **3** | Third Button | Press the Green button |
| **4** | Fourth & Fifth | Press the Red button twice |
| **5** | Eleventh Button | Press the Red button |
| **6** | Activate Machine | Place the resulting Black Shard in the machine and press the small red button |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Smelt the Ore">
    Locate the black ore within the cave chamber and place it into the left furnace to begin the smelting process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Input the Sequence">
    Interact with the control panel and press the colored buttons in the exact sequence: Blue (2nd), Green (3rd), and Red (4th and 5th).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Insert the Shard">
    Retrieve the finalized black shard from the furnace, place it directly into the main machine, and press the small red button.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Align the Mirror">
    Align the newly created Darchlight Mirror toward the glowing green shard to reflect the pure light and unlock the exit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
