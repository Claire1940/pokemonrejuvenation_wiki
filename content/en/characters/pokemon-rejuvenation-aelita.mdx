<Section icon={Swords} title="Battle Strategies & Partner Synergy">

When fighting alongside Aelita in multi-battles, understanding her team composition is crucial. She primarily utilizes Fighting-type Pokémon, which offer immense physical offensive pressure but suffer from common weaknesses to Psychic, Flying, and Fairy-type attacks. 

To maximize your efficiency in joint battles, your team should focus on covering these vulnerabilities while taking advantage of the active Field Effects. For instance, in the Sheridan Arena, Fighting-type moves receive a substantial power boost. By bringing support Pokémon that can set up screens, control speed, or neutralize her counters, you can allow her sweepers to clean up the opposing field.
