<Section icon={Swords} title="pokemon rejuvenation griselda Battle Strategies">

Defeating **pokemon rejuvenation griselda** requires more than just type advantages. Her team is built around **Field Effect** synergy, often using the field to mitigate her weaknesses while doubling the damage of her sweepers. In V14, her AI is programmed to switch aggressively if you attempt to set up, meaning you must lead with momentum-swinging moves like U-Turn or Volt Switch.

The battle typically takes place on a field that enhances **Psychic** and **Ghost** types. If you do not change the field, her lead Pokémon will likely outspeed and one-shot your attackers. Using Pokémon with the **Drizzle** or **Drought** abilities can help, but the most effective method is using moves that specifically "short-circuit" the field.
