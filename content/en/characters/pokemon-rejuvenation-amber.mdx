- **Pokemon Rejuvenation Amber** is the 8th Gym Leader of the Aevium Region, specializing in **Fire-type** Pokemon.
- Players encounter her early in the **Aquamarine Cave**, but her official Gym battle takes place much later at the **Teila Resort**.
- Defeating her rewards the trainer with the **Lyric Badge** (formerly the Flare Badge) and allows further story progression.

  <Step num="1" title="Aquamarine Cave Dispute">
    Amber is first encountered in the Aquamarine Cave. She demands the player retrieve her stolen CD from **Venam**. This encounter highlights the long-standing animosity between the two, rooted in the Gearen City fire incident.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Valor Mountain Crisis">
    At Valor Mountain, Amber's mother Tesla and the antagonist Madelis are swallowed by a **Kyogre**. After the player defeats the legendary Pokemon, Amber reconciles with her mother, marking a turning point in her spoiled behavior.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Teila Resort Gym Battle">
    Amber invites the player to her "party" at the Teila Resort, which is actually her Gym. You must increase the "hype" of the venue by battling trainers and interacting with the crowd before she will accept your challenge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="GDC Tournament Referee">
    In later chapters, Amber takes on a professional role as the commentator and referee for the **Grand Dream City Tournament**, showing her increased sense of responsibility within the Aevium League.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Amber Gym Battle Strategies">

The battle against **pokemon rejuvenation amber** is notorious for its high offensive pressure. As a Fire-type specialist, she utilizes field effects to maximize her damage output. The battle typically takes place on the **Volcanic Top Field** or the **Concert Venue**, both of which offer different advantages.

| Pokemon | Type | Primary Role | Key Moves |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ninetales** | Fire | Weather Setter | Drought, Fire Blast, Solar Beam |
| **Blaziken** | Fire/Fighting | Physical Sweeper | Speed Boost, High Jump Kick, Blaze Kick |
| **Chandelure** | Ghost/Fire | Special Attacker | Shadow Ball, Overheat, Energy Ball |
| **Rotom-Heat** | Electric/Fire | Utility/Pivot | Volt Switch, Overheat, Will-O-Wisp |
| **Taloflame** | Fire/Flying | Priority Attacker | Gale Wings, Brave Bird, Flare Blitz |
| **Magmortar** | Fire | Heavy Hitter | Thunderbolt, Fire Blast, Focus Blast |

<Callout type="warning" title="Field Effect Danger">
On the **Volcanic Top Field**, Fire-type moves receive a massive 1.5x damage boost, and Water-type moves are weakened. Using moves like **Rain Dance** or **Blizzard** can help mitigate this disadvantage, though Amber's Ninetales will attempt to reset the sun immediately.
</Callout>

### Recommended Counters
To defeat Amber effectively, players should focus on high-speed Rock and Ground types, or bulky Water types that can survive a boosted hit.

- **Rock Types**: Lycanroc or Tyranitar can provide Sandstorm weather to cancel her Sun and deal 4x damage to Talonflame.
- **Thick Fat Users**: Pokemon like Azumarill or Hariyama with the **Thick Fat** ability are excellent for soaking up Fire-type attacks.
- **Flash Fire**: Using your own Flash Fire Pokemon (like Arcanine or Houndoom) can allow you to switch into her attacks and gain a power boost.

### Fun Facts
- The name **Amber** refers to a yellow-orange fossilized resin, but also a color associated with conventional fires.
- In earlier versions of *Pokemon Rejuvenation*, Venam actually pushed Amber down a mountain during their argument.
- Amber is a playable character during the **Nightmare Realm** segment, allowing players to use her specialized team directly.
- Her favorite hobby, besides battling, is playing her bright red electric guitar, which she inherited from Tesla's band days.
