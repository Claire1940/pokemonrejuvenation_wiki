To successfully complete all Sheridan-based **pokemon rejuvenation side quests**, you must follow specific sequences. Missing a step or progressing the main story too quickly can temporarily lock you out of these rewards.

### The Revamped Hidden Library

To begin this quest, speak with Karen in East Gearen Park. She will direct you to the old Jenes Factory. Inside, you will find the entrance to the newly designed Hidden Library. 

The library features a unique vertical layout. Many players get stuck here because they do not realize that the large bookshelves act as ladders. Face any wooden bookshelf with vertical gaps and press the action button to climb up or down. The area is filled with wild Unowns, which serve as an excellent source of early-game experience points. Collect the scattered colored books in the central room to trigger an encounter with a level 25 Ghost-type variant Elgyem.

### Stolen Cargo & Field Swapping

Speak to Venom in Sheridan Village to initiate this quest. This mission serves as an interactive tutorial for the game's complex Field Effect system. You will enter a factory where security grids block your path. 

To bypass these grids, you must engage in a wild battle near the generators. Use an Electric-type move like Discharge or Spark. This action swaps the active field to an Electric Field, short-circuiting the security systems and allowing you to walk freely. Help Venom defeat the factory guards to secure TM Venoshock.

### Escaping the Kecleon Shop

The Kecleon quest on Route 2 is a direct homage to the Pokémon Mystery Dungeon series. You are tasked with retrieving a Mirror L from the Kecleon Shop. While you can purchase it for an exorbitant price, stealing it is the intended path for optimal rewards. However, stealing triggers an alarm, and level 100 Kecleons will pursue you. To escape successfully, you must navigate the backrooms using a precise sequence of doors.

The combat encounters tied to these side quests require careful preparation, especially if you are playing on higher difficulty settings. 

### Battle Request 2: Defeating Novie & Roren

This quest only triggers at night in Sheridan Village. You will team up with Roren in a double battle against Novie. Novie's team focuses heavily on speed control and field manipulation.

Novie's lead Pokémon will spam Icy Wind to lower your team's speed. To counter this, lead with a Pokémon that possesses the Inner Focus ability or use priority moves like Fake Out. Her Charjabug has high physical defense, making special attacks like Ember or Confusion highly effective. Watch out for Rain Dance setups, which boost her Water-type moves. Utilize Electric-type or Grass-type moves to quickly dispatch her Seaking and Flaaffy. Defeating her rewards you with a level 25 Clobbopus, an excellent Fighting-type addition to your PC.

### Catching the Route 2 Solrock

During the "Missing Boyfriend" quest, you must locate a lost trainer on Route 2 who is being terrorized by a wild Solrock. To complete the quest, you must capture the Solrock rather than knock it out. 

Solrock possesses the Levitate ability, making Ground-type moves useless. Use status-inflicting moves like Nuzzle, Thunder Wave, or Sleep Powder to immobilize it. Lower its HP using Grass, Water, or Ghost-type moves, but be careful not to trigger a critical hit that faints it. Once caught, return to the Sheridan Inn to claim a massive cash reward of 8,000 PokeDollars.
