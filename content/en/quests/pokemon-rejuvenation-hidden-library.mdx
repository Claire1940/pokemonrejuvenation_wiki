<Section icon={Target} title="Initiating the Investigation">

To begin your journey, head to the **Gearen Help Plaza**. This hub serves as the central location for most sidequests in the city. Interact with the help board to officially accept the request titled "The Hidden Library." Once accepted, your objective is to locate the client who requested the investigation.

The client is a Hiker situated within the **Gearen Laboratory**. This building is a landmark in the city, but the Hiker is tucked away in a specific room, so follow these steps to find him quickly:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Gearen Laboratory">
    Locate the large Laboratory building in Gearen City and head inside.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach the Second Floor">
    Take the stairs or elevator to the 2nd floor of the facility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the Hiker">
    Enter the room on the right side of the hallway. Speak to the Hiker standing there to learn about the "missing" library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">

<Section icon={Map} title="Locating the Witnesses">

After speaking with the Hiker, he will task you with gathering information from three specific individuals around Gearen City. These NPCs are scattered across different districts, requiring a bit of a trek through the urban landscape. Look for the "!" icon above their heads to confirm they are the correct quest targets.

| Witness NPC | District Location | Precise Spot |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Lady** | North Gearen | Just north of the Gearen Laboratory entrance |
| **Karrina** | Southeast Docks | Standing near the water in the dock area |
| **Receptionist** | Northeast Gearen | Behind the desk at the Chrisola Hotel |

Each NPC provides a piece of the puzzle regarding why the library is no longer visible in the city. It is recommended to visit the Lady first as she is closest to the Lab, then head east to the Hotel, and finally south to the Docks.

<Callout type="success" title="Exploration Bonus">
While visiting the docks to speak with Karrina, check the nearby trash cans and hidden corners. Gearen City is full of hidden items like Potions and Antidotes that are incredibly helpful for your first few gym battles.
</Callout>

</Section>
