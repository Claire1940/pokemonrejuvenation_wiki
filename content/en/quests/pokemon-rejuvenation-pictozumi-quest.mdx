<Section icon={Target} title="Phase 1: Early Game Captures">

The first phase of the **pokemon rejuvenation pictozumi quest** focuses on Pokemon found in the earlier routes of the game. However, even these "common" Pokemon have specific encounter conditions that can frustrate unprepared trainers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Capture Dedenne on Route 2">
    Your first task is to find a Dedenne. It is located on **Route 2** but only appears during the **Daytime** or **Morning**. It has a relatively low encounter rate, so be prepared to spend a few minutes in the tall grass.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Drifloon in Wispy Path">
    Next, Zumi requests a Drifloon. Head to the **Wispy Path** (near Golden Leaf Town). Drifloon is a **Nighttime** exclusive encounter. Players have reported spending 8-10 minutes of continuous hunting to trigger this encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate Pyukumuku in Terajuma">
    The third target is Pyukumuku. This is significantly easier as it is a **static encounter**. Travel to **Terajuma Island** and check the beach areas near **Terajuma Resort** or **Kakuri**. You can see them interacting with the environment on the sand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Pokemon | Location | Time of Day | Encounter Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dedenne** | Route 2 | Morning/Day | Wild Grass |
| **Drifloon** | Wispy Path | Night | Wild Grass |
| **Pyukumuku** | Terajuma Resort | Any | Static Overworld |

<Callout type="tip" title="Time Manipulation">
If you are playing on a PC, you can manually adjust your system clock to trigger Morning (8:00 AM) or Night (12:00 AM) encounters without waiting in real-time.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Phase 2: The Lurking Lighthouse and West Gearen">

As the quest progresses, the requirements shift from simple wild captures to completing specific sub-quests and exploring industrial areas.

The fourth Pokemon requested is **Mareep**. Unlike previous steps, Mareep is not found in the wild during this stage of the game. You must complete the **Lurking Lighthouse** sidequest available at the Terajuma Help Center. Upon completion, you are rewarded with a special **Aevian Mareep**. Zumi will accept the data for either the standard form or the Aevian form.

Following Mareep, you must find **Toxel**. Toxel is located in the **West Gearen City Factory** area. This involves navigating the elevator systems in the industrial district. Toxel does not have a specific time requirement, making it easier to hunt once you reach the sewers/factory sub-level.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Phase 3: Rare Aevian Variants">

The final phase of the **pokemon rejuvenation pictozumi quest** involves hunting down rare Aevian variants that are notoriously difficult to find.

**Aevian Budew** is located in the **Badlands**. This encounter is strictly limited to the **Morning**. Even during the correct time, the encounter rate is extremely low. Trainers often spend several minutes cycling through encounters before this variant appears. 

The final target is **Aevian Litwick**, found within the **Access High Factory** at Access High University. This area is only accessible after progressing through the East Gearen City Neo story arc and completing the related campus quests.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Final Rewards and Completion">

Once you have shown Zumi all seven Pokemon, she will conclude the quest and provide the ultimate prize: the **Ampharosite**. This Mega Stone allows a Jotoan Ampharos to Mega Evolve, providing a massive boost in Special Attack and defensive capabilities.

After the quest is "Case Closed," Zumi moves from her temporary location to her permanent spot in the **Gearen Lab**. You can find her there in the later stages of the game, where she continues her duties as a developer NPC.
