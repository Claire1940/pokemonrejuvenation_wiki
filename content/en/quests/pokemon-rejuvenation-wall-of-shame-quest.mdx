<Section icon={Map} title="Overview of the pokemon rejuvenation wall of shame quest">

The **pokemon rejuvenation wall of shame quest** is a highly popular mid-game sidequest available at the Kakori Village Help Center on Terajuma Island. This quest tasks players with solving a mystery involving the vandalism of the local "Wall of Shame," a bulletin board where the village posts the names of troublemakers and debtors. Completing this quest is essential for players aiming to maximize their Achievement Points (AP) and unlock high-tier rewards in V14.

To begin, players must have progressed to Chapter 5 and gained access to Kakori Village. The quest is posted on the local Help Center board and requires careful exploration of the surrounding jungle. It serves as an excellent introduction to the region's environmental storytelling and unique field mechanics.
